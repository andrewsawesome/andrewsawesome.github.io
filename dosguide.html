A Beginner's Guide to MS-DOS

What is MS-DOS?

MS-DOS is an abbreviation for Microsoft Disk Operating System. This tutorial will be in referance to MS-DOS 6.22, the last standalone version of MS-DOS. You can get MS-DOS 6.22 here.

What was the purpose of MS-DOS?

The purpose of MS-DOS was to provide a simple, lightweight operating system that could be booted off a floppy disk. After the booting process, the floppy could then be removed.

What are some commands I can use in MS-DOS

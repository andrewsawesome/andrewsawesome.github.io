How to enable 2 Factor Authentication on a Computer

Note: To follow this guide you must download Microsoft Authenticatior or a similar app on your phone

Amazon

1. Go to amazon.com or smile.amazon.com

2. Click "Account"(in the top left corner)

3. Click "Login and Security"

4. Click "Turn on" next to Two-Step Verification

5. Go into the Microsoft Authenticator app, and click "Add account"

6. On the main screen of the Microsoft Authenticator app, click on the Amazon account you just added

7. Enter the One-Time password on the Amazon website

Google

1. Go to myaccount.google.com

2. At the top, click "Security".

3. Scroll down to "Signing in to Google"

4. Click "2-Step Verification"

5. Follow the steps to turn on 2-step Verification

Microsoft

1. Go to account.microsoft.com

2. Click "Security"

3. Click "Advanced Security Options"

4. Click "Turn on" next to Two-Step Verification

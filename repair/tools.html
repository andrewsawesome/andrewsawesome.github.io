iFixit Pro Tech Toolkit($69.99)

The iFixit Pro Tech Toolkit isn't just a screwdriver set. It takes the Mako driver kit and adds a jimmy tool, tweezers, reverse tweezers, a spudger, and a suction. This is, in my opinion, all you need to repair any electronics. Just make sure to be careful with the reveerse tweezers.

Essential Electronics Toolkit($24.99)

This kit contains the Minnow driver kit, jimmy tool, suction cup, spudgers, and pics. The drivers of note here are the Sim Eject, Torx Security, Pentalobe, Phillips, Tri-wing and Flathead bits. It does not contain a flex extension, hex bits or sockets. It also does not contain some specialty bits, like Gamebit, Triangle, or Spanner(the flathead filled in in the middle) bits.

Runner-Up

Oria Repair Kit($25.99)

This kit contains Phillips, Torx Security, Hex, Pentalobe, Tri-wing, Triangle, Square, and nut driver bits. It also has a flexible extension, pics, spudgers, a brush, and a suction cup.

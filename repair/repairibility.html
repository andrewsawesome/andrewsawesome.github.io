Repairibility of Electronics

Mobvoi Ticwatch E

Repairibility: Low

The Ticwatch E is easy to open(using a T2 screwdriver), but it's easy to mess up the screen ribbon cable, and there aren't any replacement parts. If you have a broken Ticwatch E, it's best to send it to Mobvoi at support.mobvoi.com

Apple iPhone 5

Repairibility: High

The Apple iPhone 6 is very modular, and components are easily replaceable. It also has a tab for the battery. Just keep in mind you will need a pentalobe screwdriver to open it.

Texas Instruments TI-30XS Multiview Calculator

Repairibility: Medium

Easy to open up(just remove the 6 Phillips screws on the back and pop off the back), however, the only easily replaceable component is the CR2032 battery.

Best

Zarchiver

This app extracts 7z (7zip), zip, rar, rar5, bzip2, gz, XZ, iso, tar, arj, cab, lzh, lha, lzma, xar, tgz, tbz, Z, deb, rpm, zipx, mtz, chm, dmg, cpio, cramfs, img (fat, ntfs, ubf), wim, ecm, lzip, zst (zstd), egg, and alz files. It also lets you create 7z, zip, bz2, gz, XZ, lz4, tar, and zst (zstd) archives.

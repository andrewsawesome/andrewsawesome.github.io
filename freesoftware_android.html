Archiving/Dearchiving Utilities

Best

Zarchiver

Without ads, this app extracts 7z (7zip), zip, rar, rar5, bzip2, gz, XZ, iso, tar, arj, cab, lzh, lha, lzma, xar, tgz, tbz, Z, deb, rpm, zipx, mtz, chm, dmg, cpio, cramfs, img (fat, ntfs, ubf), wim, ecm, lzip, zst (zstd), egg, and alz files. It also lets you create 7z, zip, bz2, gz, XZ, lz4, tar, and zst (zstd) archives.

Runner-Up

RAR

Although it has adverstisements in the app, RAR lets you extract RAR, ZIP, TAR, GZ, BZ2, XZ, 7z, ISO, ARJ archives. It also lets you create RAR and ZIP archives

Video playback

Best

VLC for Android

This app lets you play back MKV, MP4, AVI, MOV, Ogg, FLAC, TS, M2TS, Wv and AAC files.

How much Faster much faster is is to use a coin sorter than sorting by hand?

I know that coin sorters are faster than hand sorting coins, but just how much faster are they than sorting by hand? I ran some tests. In Test 1, I have $3.03 worth of change. In test 2, I have $8.65 worth of change. I mixed both batches of coins between each test.

Test 1 Results

Using a Coin Sorter:30 Seconds

Sorting By Hand: 1 minute and 17 seconds

In this test the coin sorter was 47 seconds faster.

Test 2 Resulults

Note: This batch contained mostly quarters

Coin Sorter: 15 seconds

Sorting By Hand: 33 seconds

In this test, the coin sorter was 18 seconds faster

The coin sorter used in this test was a Royal Soveriegn QS-1 Manual Coin Sorter

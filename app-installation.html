Installing my app

First, Follow this link, then click "download"

On Kindle Fire

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap "Security & Privacy"
  3. Tap "Apps from unknown sources", then tap "ok" on the folllowing dialog box
  4. Open Silk Browser
  5. Tap the three dots at the top right, then tap "Downloads".
  6. Find "app-release.apk" and tap on it.
  7. Tap "install". When the install is finished, tap "open".

On Android

  1. If you are signed in to Google Drive, you will need to choose an account to continue. Choose an account, then click "ok"
  2. You will now see a popup. Tap settings and toggle on "Allow from this source", then tap the back button. If you are running Android Nougat or older, toggle on "unknown sources"
  3. Tap "install". When the install is finished, tap "open".

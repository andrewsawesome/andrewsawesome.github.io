Services At Calming Rehab Substance Abuse Center

Tennis Courts

Our indoor tennis courts are open 24/7 with tennis balls and tennis rackets.

Movie Watching

Watch a movie at our movie watching area, open 8AM-9PM everyday, with chairs, TVs, DVD players, and a large collection of movies.

Pool(Summer Only)

We have a large outdoor pool open in the summer everyday from 10AM-5PM.

Yoga Classes

We have indoor yoga classes from 5-6PM Monday-Friday.

Essential Oil Diffusers In Every Room

We have essential Oil Diffusers in every patient's room. This helps them calm and de-stress without using drugs, which may help them get off drugs. A lot of people use drugs as a way to take out stress in thr=eir life, but this provides a much more natural, un-damaging way of doing that.

Soothing Sounds

Soothing sounds are available via either the oil diffuser or a seperate soothing sounds machine
